About Me

Hi everyone. I'll tell you a little about myself: I am a lawyer by profession, but when I emigrated to Argentina in 2017, I decided to turn my career around and leaned towards technology, specifically Front End Web Development, always trying to maintain good programming practices and constant learning.

Currently, I work as an IT Support Analyst at Dentsu International, maintaining and updating the content of 141 websites of an important company in the automotive industry, all this in conjunction with a team that includes other Developers, Graphic Designers, Copywriters and Account Executives.

Also, I work as a Professor of the Coderhouse Web Development course, which motivates me to always be up to date to provide a good learning experience for my students.

Knowledge
